Albay is a province located in the Bicol Region in southeastern Luzon of the Philippines. Its capital is the City of Legazpi. Tourists know the province more as
home to the spectacular and famous Mount Mayon Volcano, the crown jewel of the region and among the great wonders of the world because of its
perfect cone shape.
